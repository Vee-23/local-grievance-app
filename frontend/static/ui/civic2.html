Frequently Asked Questions

How does the reporting process work?

Simply take a photo of the civic issue, mark its location on the map, provide a brief description, and submit. Our AI system automatically categorizes and routes your complaint to the appropriate department.

Can I report issues anonymously?

Yes! You can choose to submit reports anonymously. However, creating an account allows you to track submissions and receive updates on their status.
